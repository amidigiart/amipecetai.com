The 6 analysis pillars

Each pillar detects a different dimension of manipulation. Together, they create a complete picture.

Deep semantics

We analyze the real meaning, not just the words. Euphemisms, hidden implications, presuppositions, subtext.

  • Absolutist language patterns

Intent & motivation

We detect the real purpose behind the message. Declared vs actual goal, implicit interests, real beneficiary.

Emotional manipulation

We identify emotional influence techniques. Fear, guilt, flattery, false urgency — exposed.

Disinformation & distortions

We spot false or distorted information. Factual falsehoods, omissions, cherry picking, unsourced claims.

  • Abusive generalizations

  • Unsourced factual claims

Logic & congruence

We verify coherence and consistency. Internal contradictions, logic leaps, non sequiturs, double standards.

  • Internal contradictions
  • Logic leaps without premises
  • Non sequitur arguments
  • Double standards

Context & hidden agenda

We reconstruct the real context and agenda. Framing, tribalism, deflection, victimization patterns.
